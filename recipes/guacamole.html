Guacamole

How to Make Guacamole

This easy guacamole recipe is quick and simple to make. Great with tortilla chips or as a topping for Mexican foods!

Guacamole ingredients:
-Avocados:
This easy guacamole recipe starts with two peeled and pitted avocados.
-Onions and tomatoes:
Chopped onions and tomatoes add flavor, color, and texture.
-Seasonings:
This flavorful guacamole is seasoned with fresh garlic, salt, and pepper.
-Lime juice:
Fresh lime juice lends flavor and keeps the guacamole from browning.

How to Make Guacamole Step-By-Step

Steps:

  1. Mash avocado in a medium serving bowl.
  2. Stir in onion, tomato, and garlic.
  3. Season with lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Cover and chill guacamole for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
